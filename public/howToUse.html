How to use:

Creating an Event
1.) Give your event a name
2.) Select a potential date range for your event
3.) Select a start time, will be displayed in military
4.) Select an end time, will be displayed in military
5.) Select "Create Event", you will be redirected to your events viewing / planning page
6.) Share the link to the viewing / planning page with your team

Adding and Viewing Availablity
1.) Select the times you are available (They will be highlighted in blue)
2.) To unselect a time, click it again
3.) To submit your availabiltiy click "add my availability"
4.) That's it! revist this page to view everyone's availability
